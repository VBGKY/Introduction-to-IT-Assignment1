Top 10 Grossing Arcade Games

Number 1:Pacman

Pacman is one of the most iconic and well known arcade games in history. Since its release in the United States in 1980, approximentally 400,000 Units have been sold. Due to the boom in popularity, the revenue generated by 1985 was a heafty $3.5 billion USD. For a more information and an indepth look into Pacman, head to the Pacman page.

Number 2:Space Invaiders

Space Invaiders was developed by Taito and released in 1979. This arcade game has be credited for a the era of gaming known as the 'Golden Age of Arcades'. With 360,000 cabinets sold and a revenue of $2.7 billion USD generated by 1982, this iconic shooting style arcade game deserves second place.

Number 3:Street Fighter II/Champion Edition

Street Fighter II/Champion Edition (released in 1992) is the sequal to the popular Arcade Game Street Fighter (released in 1987 ), with the champion edition specifically being one of various updates for Street Fighter II. This version of the well known 90's combat game has sold 200,000 units and produced $2.3 billion by 1995.
